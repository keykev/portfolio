BIO

I'm Kevin Yang. I graduated from University of California Riverside in the winter of December 2019 with a Bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering. In my sophmore year of college, I was introduced to programming as a problem solving tool. From then on, I was drawn to it like a moth drawn to a flame.

This drive led me to look into different programming careers in my freetime, and it was where I discover web development. I was hooked! Subsequently, over the years, I built various projects with HTML, CSS, and Javascript ranging from responsive websites, to games on the browser, and to implementing APIs.

After graduating, I obtained my Engineer-in-Training license and am now working on my goal to be a front end developer. Even now, my drive to continiously explore and improve my skills on various technologies is still going strong.
